School Activites

Our vibrant array of activities provides students with opportunities for personal growth, skill development, and a memorable school experience.

Birthday celebration

We believe in making every student feel special on their birthday and creating joyful memories.

Dussehra

Dussehra is the symbol of triumph over adversity, teaching our students the values of courage and righteousness.

Halloween

Halloween is a time for creative fun, fostering community spirit and imagination among our students.

Independence Day

Independence Day is a momentous celebration, marking our collective freedom and fostering unity among our students.

Christmas

Christmas is a festive occasion that brings joy and warmth, fostering a spirit of togetherness.

Tiger's Day

A special day dedicated to raising awareness about the sense of environmental responsibility in our students.

Summer Camp Activities

Our summer camp offers a diverse range of engaging and educational activities, ensuring an unforgettable and enriching experience for students during the break

Green Day

We dedicate a day to emphasize the importance of environmental conservation, teaching our students about the planet.

Rainy Day

We turn a rainy day into a creative and fun experience for our students, fostering adaptability and a love for the beauty of nature.

Lemon Day

An exciting occasion where students discover the versatility of lemons, while embracing the importance of diversity and individuality.

Navratri

Our school's Navratri festivities teach students about India's rich culture through dance, music, and vibrant traditional attire.
